Plunger Lift Optimization Training Simulator

Physics-based plunger lift simulator for cycle optimization and training using backpressure IPR, force balance plunger dynamics, and Turner critical velocity. Configured for a conventional barstock plunger on a 7,000 ft TVD depleted gas well.

The controller mirrors RTU logic with configurable open/close triggers, load factor, timers, and close delay. The process variables update in real time next to setpoints.

The well is configured as a representative gas well producing approximately 150-200 Mcf/day when optimized.

Use the speed control to fast-forward. Click any label for a definition.
